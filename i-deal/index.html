Become part of the booming mattress and furniture business as the newest of nearly three hundred iDeal Furniture Distribution Center (DC) owners! No experience required and training is provided at no extra cost. Work part-time or full-time, or even by appointment only. Fast startup time, low startup costs, small overhead and high margins make this an attractive option to anyone interested in starting their own business!

As an iDeal Furniture Distribution Center (DC) owner, you will have access to iDeal Furniture's nationwide buying power for low wholesale pricing, plus access to a growing network of complementary businesses that can help you gain customers you wouldn't reach otherwise.

You'll get help recruiting your own network of “FurnitureBROKERS” to help supercharge your business, plus assistance with licensing, financing, branding, and other aspects of getting your business up and running.

This is a low-risk business model with very good income potential.

Key Advantages

  • Fast startup; you could be taking a salary within three months of coming on board!
  • Most DC owners operate by appointment only in their spare time, but you can go full-time if you wish to boost earning potential
  • No prior experience required; training provided at no extra cost
  • Low initial investment and very low overhead
  • Get the buying power that comes from being part of a nationwide network
  • National team of furnitureBrokers exists to help drive business to you
  • Marketing materials and training provided
  • High margins mean you can beat the big guys on both price and service, while still making great money! 

Want more? Ask about licensing extra territory for passive income!

In addition to running your own DC, you may choose to license a territory with sufficient area or population to support multiple Distribution Centers. Your own DC can provide quick income, then your passive income grows as you build the iDeal Furniture network within your territory!
